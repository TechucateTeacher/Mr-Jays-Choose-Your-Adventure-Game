High School Story

It's the first week of second semester. The school has begun to crackdown on rule breakers, and you know at any moment you could be the next one called to the principal's office. The last class of the day has begun and you're fighting sleep and boredom. Your best friend Gio is behind you trying to get your attention.

You ignore Gio, so he tries to throw wadded up paper in your direction. Annoyed, you almost turn around, but at the last second, the teacher sees Gio ready to throw another piece of paper. Gio is now at the mercy of the principal... That was close!

You turn around to see what Gio wants. He says to check your phone. You pull out your phone to find a funny meme in your messages and you bust out laughing. The teacher turns around from his lesson and glares at both of you. You both have been sent to the Principal's Office. Game Over.

After a close call, you take a deep breath and start to focus. There's still 45 minutes left in class and you start to feel how long that time really is. You look around and see a couple of people with their heads down or their earbuds in. You're unsure if doing either is a good idea, but if you could, you'd sleep for the rest of class. Surely enough, your start to nod off.

You fight to stay awake, but you don't know how much longer you can take...

You cave and allow your head to rest for a second and accidentally fall asleep. The teacher tries to wake you up for 5 minutes before finally, you wake up. The teacher asks you to answer a question, but you fail to give an answer. You and a few others have been sent to the Principal's Office. Game Over.

Class is finally over. You get your freedom once you walk through those doors. You did it. The sun looks bright through the door windows, the hall is filled with noise of closing lockers and scattered footsteps. Why do I hear an alarm clock ringing...

You're suddenly awakened by your angry mother. You SLEPT IN! You're going to be late and you're definitely getting lunch detention now...
